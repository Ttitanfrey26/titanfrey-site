Welcome

Step into the world of Titan Frey, where stories echo with emotion, resilience, and legacy. Explore books, scripts, articles, short films, and YouTube videos that illuminate the human experience through bold narrative and quiet strength.

Titan Frey was formerly the editor-in-chief, journalist, and social media specialist for fadeawayworld.net, a basketball website. He is now a co-owner and journalist for the basketball website Ballexclusives.com.

Beyond his work with sports websites, Titan is an author with over ten published books and a screenwriter with three produced short films. He has co-written a video game, performed copywriting work, and crafted YouTube scripts for various clients, including Oldskoolbball, a popular YouTube channel that has collaborated with NBA.com, ESPN, and The Last Dance Documentary.

Additionally, Titan writes for his two personal YouTube channels, one focused on basketball and the other on fictional stories.

An interesting fact about Titan is that he is a living kidney donor, having donated to a friend in 2012.
